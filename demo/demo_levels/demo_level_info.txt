* Do not use extra space or characters *

Format:

ObjectType,x,y,width,height

ObjectType: either DynamicObject or StaticObject
x: x coordinate position, must be float (int)
y: y coordinate position, must be float (int)
width: object width, must be float (int)
height: object height, must be float (int)
